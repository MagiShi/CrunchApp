Home Page and Searching

When you first arrive at the Home page, you see a list of all the items in the system. You can narrow down the list by using the Filter Menu. You can also search for a specific item by typing its name or its unique barcode into the search bar.

Filter Menu

You can access the Filter Menu by clicking on the Filter Menu icon (  ) next to the search bar.

Open up the different panels (e.g. "Color," "Size") to specify which characteristics you wish to narrow down the list by. The default state, where all the checkboxes under each panel are unchecked, means the list is not filtered by any of those characteristics. For example, if the Size panel has no checkboxes checked, then you are viewing items of all sizes.

After you check the appropriate checkboxes, make sure to press the Apply Filters button in order to refresh the list of items with the filters now applied.

How to Distinguish Identical Items

What if there are many similar, but still different, brown pants in the application? Even worse, what if there are two of the exact same item, the only difference being their sizes?

For the former case, this issue can be avoided by making sure to give items descriptive names. For the latter case, you will have to find specific items by looking up their barcode; even if you have multiple pairs of the exact same brown pants, each listing will still have a unique barcode.

Barcodes

When you add a new item, our application automatically generates a unique barcode for that item. No two items should ever have the same barcode.

An item's barcode cannot be changed, so make sure to label the physical item carefully with its correct barcode.

Item Reservations

If you wish to have full possession of an item for a defined period of time, you can mark the item as reserved. To do so, go into the specific item's Item Detail page and click the Reserve Item button near the top of the screen.

On the Reserve Item page, select a date range using the calendar widget. Dates that are gray and have a strikethrough are unavailable. First click the date you want the reservation to start, and then select the date you want the reservation to end.

Once a reservation has been successfully made, you will be able to view it, along with all your other reservations, on the My Reservations page. The link to the page is under the right side menu with the icon. The reservations are organized by whether they are current/ongoing reservations, future/upcoming reservations, or past reservations. You can edit the date range of upcoming reservations or delete them entirely. Make sure to press the Apply button when editing the date range of upcoming reservations!
